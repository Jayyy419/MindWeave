# Proposal Improvement Draft (5-Page Max)

This draft is structured for the AI Ready ASEAN Youth Challenge written proposal constraints:

- PDF, English
- Maximum 5 pages
- Arial 12
- 1.5 line spacing

## Page Allocation (Recommended)

1. Page 1: Problem and Beneficiaries
2. Page 2: AI Solution Design
3. Page 3: Feasibility and Viability
4. Page 4: Scalability, Sustainability, and Impact Model
5. Page 5: Community Outreach Plan (1,000 people)

## Page 1 - Problem and Beneficiaries

## Objective

Define the real-world challenge in ASEAN context with clear beneficiary focus.

## Content Checklist

- Problem statement in one paragraph with ASEAN-specific context.
- 2-4 data points (regional and country-level where possible).
- Primary beneficiary group definition (one sentence with age, context, and need).
- Secondary beneficiary groups (optional, max 2).
- Why current alternatives are insufficient.

## Suggested Wording Template

"Across ASEAN, many youth face rising stress and low access to timely mental wellbeing support. Existing options are often expensive, stigmatized, or difficult to access consistently. MindWeave addresses this gap by offering an accessible, AI-supported emotional reflection and coping platform focused on [primary beneficiary]."

## Page 2 - AI Solution Design

## Objective

Show exactly how AI is integrated and why it improves outcomes.

## Content Checklist

- Solution overview in plain language.
- AI components and their purpose:
  - Reframing support
  - Personalized learning progression
  - Risk-aware support prompts
- Input -> AI process -> user output flow.
- Responsible AI safeguards (risk detection, disclaimers, escalation resources).

## Suggested Diagram

- One architecture flow diagram: User Input -> AI Layer -> Safety Layer -> Output + Progress Tracking.

## Page 3 - Feasibility and Viability

## Objective

Convince judges this can operate in practice now, not just conceptually.

## Content Checklist

- Current implementation status and what is already functional.
- Technical architecture summary and deployment status.
- Pilot execution plan (90 days).
- Key operational assumptions and risk controls.
- Team capability and delivery plan.

## Suggested KPI Table

- Signups
- Weekly active users
- Lesson completion rate
- Day-7 retention
- User-reported coping confidence delta

## Page 4 - Scalability, Sustainability, and Impact

## Objective

Show growth potential and measurable public good.

## Content Checklist

- Scalability model across schools, universities, NGOs, youth groups.
- Localization strategy across ASEAN languages and contexts.
- Sustainability model:
  - Partnerships
  - Low-cost delivery
  - Governance and moderation
- Impact model with measurable outcomes:
  - Awareness outcomes
  - Behavior change outcomes
  - Engagement outcomes

## Impact Statement Template

"Within 12 months, MindWeave targets [X] active youth users, [Y]% completion of core coping modules, and measurable improvement in self-reported coping confidence by [Z]% among participating cohorts."

## Page 5 - Outreach Plan to Reach 1,000 Community Members

## Objective

Present a practical and measurable outreach plan.

## Content Checklist

- Outreach target: at least 1,000 community members.
- Channel mix with numeric assumptions:
  - Campus workshops
  - Community partners/NGOs
  - Digital campaigns
  - Ambassador referrals
- Timeline (by month/week).
- Funnel and measurement approach.
- KPI ownership and reporting cadence.

## Outreach Math Example

- 10 campus workshops x 50 participants = 500
- 5 NGO/community sessions x 60 participants = 300
- Digital campaign conversions = 300
- Total projected reach = 1,100

## Outreach Evaluation Metrics

- Reach count
- Participation rate
- Literacy module completion
- Follow-up engagement rate

## Rubric Alignment Summary (Add as a final box)

- Problem definition: addressed in Page 1
- Relevancy and impact: Pages 1 and 4
- AI application: Page 2
- Viability: Page 3
- Innovation: Pages 2 and 4
- Sustainability: Page 4
- Outreach practicality/effectiveness: Page 5

## Optional Visual Assets (Recommended)

- One wireframe sheet
- One impact dashboard screenshot
- One outreach funnel chart

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Overly broad beneficiaries with no clear segment.
- Claims without measurable targets.
- AI described abstractly without concrete user flow.
- Outreach plan without channel-level numbers.
- No risk/safety section for responsible AI.
